roots rock with feeling

Wake Up John is a husband/wife/husband/wife quartet from Lancaster Pennsylvania blending alternative rock, folk, americana, and blues into an eclectic but familiar style that is sure to get your feet tapping. Nathan, Hannah, Hannah and Travis pair catchy melodies with precise harmonies to bring powerful emotion out of simple, memorable original songs.

With members raised in Amish country (PA), maple syrup country (VT), and wine country (CA), Wake Up John proves that defying genre limitations doesn't mean you have to abandon fun.
